Intro
Thabe Moyo Constructions & Projects (Pty) Ltd is a black empowerment corporation formed in 2013. Thabe Moyo Constructions & Projects in association is a 100% BEE company that is rendering an excellent and dedicated service to its clients. We are a company with qualified and experienced employees operating within the bounds of our expertise.
Vision
To be the best and the most preferred service provider of granite industry at all time by the public and private sector in the province.
Values
In striving to achieve our mission, we will subscribe to and live by the following values:  Placing Clients satisfaction in priority;  Delivering on specifications, on time and within budget;  Conducting business transparently, ethically and responsibly;  Reinvesting in community and businesses within our operation.
Mission
We go an extra mile in resolving assisting institutions, individuals and organization in all aspects. We service a number of professionals and public sectors. We have acknowledged that the best work is that done by skillful workers. We pride ourselves on being a best company and we believe that any professional relationship should be mutual beneficial and this is our goal for each and every client that we render our services for, without distinguishing between the size of work load and its complexity
